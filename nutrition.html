Micronutrients

Minerals and Vitamins are mincronutrients are essential and play a very important role within the body, so it's important to ensure your'e consuming the recommended daily amount to help support the body to cleanse, regulate and rebuiild itself. Let's speak about Minerals first because I don't hear enough people speaking about these little super hero's and their impactful benefits. In simple, the body is made up of 102 minerals, each mineral playing a significant role within the body. Minerals can be mainly found in fruit and vegetables which are a great way to not only source minerals but vitamins too. This is because plants are soil fed and soil is mineral rich. Minerals can also be found in fish (absorb minerals from their environments) and meat (absorb minerals from eating plants/grass) so there are plenty of ways to source minerals and include them into your diet.
