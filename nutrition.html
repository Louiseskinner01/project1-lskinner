Macronutrients

Ever heard of the saying, "You are what you eat"?


Well its true, and I think it's time we started to lead with that thought. Food is the most amaizing thing on this planet, offering us a varierty of beautiful flavours that sing to our pallets, making it a challenge to have a balanced diet and eat clean (free of the following....deep fired food, high salt, sugar and fat content, processed, fast foods etc). A balanced diet offers our body the nutrients it needs to function at an optimum level so things like hormone production, muscle repair and energy levels can replenish, as well as many other bpdily functions
