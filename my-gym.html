Tips

  • When performing Squats, Lunges, and Deadlifts, keep your chest elevated and shoulders back
  • When perforiming Lunges, keep your knees pushed out externally to prevent them from caving in and losing your form
  • Focus on breathing during exercise to ensure your muscles get a sufficient amount of oxgen
  • Go through the motions.... Dig deep and give everything youv'e got
  • Movements are either Push or Pull so if you arn't in a pulling motion then you should be pushing HARD!
